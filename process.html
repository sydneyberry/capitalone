Deliverable One: Data Visuals






Types of Passes

The bar graph on the left represents how many of each pass type were used over the quarter. Looking at this, the monthly pass was the most common pass, while the staff annual was the least used pass. There was a large range between these two types that comes out to a 80,922 pass difference. If you hover each bar, you can see the specific amount of each.

Average Duration Bikes were held

This line graph was drawn from the averages of the distance travelled each month. As shown, there is a sharp drop between August and September at the end of the traditional summer break and continues to drop throughout the winter months, and appears to be starting to pick up again around March.
